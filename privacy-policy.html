Google AdSense & Advertising
Section 04

FarPlay uses Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements on our platform. This helps us keep FarPlay completely free for all users.

How Google AdSense Works
  • Google AdSense may display advertisements on FarPlay based on your browsing history and interests
  • Google and its partners use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to FarPlay and other websites on the internet
  • Advertisements may be personalized based on your location, device, browsing behavior, and demographic information
  • Google may use data about your visits to FarPlay and other websites to show you ads for products and services that may interest you
Managing Ad Preferences
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings at https://www.google.com/settings/ads
  • You can opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
  • You can disable ad personalization through your browser's cookie settings
  • Disabling personalized ads does not remove ads from FarPlay — it makes ads less relevant to you

Note: Third-party advertising partners have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy for more details about how Google handles data in connection with AdSense.

⚠️

FarPlay does not have access to or control over the cookies that are placed by third-party advertisers. The collection and use of information by Google and other advertising partners is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Analytics Services
Section 05

FarPlay uses Google Analytics (a web analytics service by Google LLC) to help us understand how visitors use our platform. This information helps us improve FarPlay's features, performance, and user experience.

What Analytics Collects
  • Number of visitors and pageviews per day, week, and month
  • Which pages and tools are most popular on FarPlay
  • How long users spend on different sections of the platform
  • What devices, browsers, and operating systems users prefer
  • General geographic location (country and city level only)
  • Traffic sources — how users discover FarPlay (search, direct, referral)
  • User flow — how users navigate through different sections of FarPlay

Important: Analytics data is generally aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify individual users from analytics data. Google Analytics uses IP anonymization on FarPlay, which means your full IP address is never stored.

Opting Out of Analytics

You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting data about your visits by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Data Storage
Section 06

FarPlay takes a privacy-first approach to data storage, keeping as much data as possible on your own device rather than on remote servers.

Local Browser Storage (Current)
  • Code projects, project names, and saved files are stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — on your device only
  • User preferences and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • This means your data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
  • Clearing your browser data or localStorage will permanently delete your saved projects
  • Data stored in localStorage is not accessible to us or any third party
Future Cloud Storage (Planned)
  • Future account features may offer optional cloud storage for your projects and preferences
  • Cloud storage will be encrypted and stored on secure servers
  • Users will always have the option to export and delete their cloud-stored data
  • We will provide clear notice and obtain consent before implementing cloud storage features
Data Retention
  • Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics for 26 months by default
  • Contact form submissions may be retained for up to 12 months to track resolution
  • Local browser data is retained indefinitely until you clear it manually

Data Security
Section 07

The security of your data is important to us. FarPlay implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures We Use
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and FarPlay is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure Hosting: FarPlay is hosted on Netlify, which provides enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Minimal Data Collection: We follow a data minimization principle — we only collect what is necessary
  • Local-First Storage: User projects are stored locally, reducing exposure of personal data to server-side risks
  • Regular Reviews: We periodically review our security practices and update them as needed
⚠️

Important Limitation: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to use strong passwords, keep your browser updated, and avoid using FarPlay on unsecured public networks for sensitive work.

Your Rights
Section 09

As a user of FarPlay, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You may request information about what personal data we hold about you. Contact us at support@farplay.app for access requests.
  • Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
